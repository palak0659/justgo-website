About Safety

Prevention measuresPublic transport means should be only used if needed. Ticketing is not available on board. Exchange is not allowed. Do not interact with the driver. In-person citizen advice office with no physical division between employees and customers will be closed. Use of public transport is discouraged to at-risk groups (60 years and over and people with previous medical conditions). Additional measures undertaken include enhancing cleaning and disinfection protocols.

Recommendations to public transport users People with symptoms or at-risk groups (60 years and over and people with previous medical conditions): please do not use public transport. All passengers shall maintain the security distance. Wash your hands (using water and soap or hydroalcoholic gel) afterwards.
